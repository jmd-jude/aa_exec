Audience Acuity Unveils Conversational Identity Intelligence

Empowering non-technical users to activate identity insights in real time from AA's Super Identity Graph

Las Vegas, June 1, 2025

Audience Acuity, the leading identity resolution provider, today announced the launch of Conversational Identity Intelligence, a transformative addition to its Super Identity Graph (SIG) that enables marketers and business strategists to interact with complex identity data through natural language. This industry-first capability eliminates the technical barriers that have traditionally limited identity data insights to SQL-proficient analysts, democratizing access to the company's industry-leading identity resolution platform.

With Conversational Identity Intelligence, we're fundamentally changing how enterprises interact with identity data. By removing technical barriers, we're empowering strategic marketers to directly transform our unparalleled identity data into business outcomes without dependency on data teams. This represents a paradigm shift from identity as a technical asset to identity as a strategic advantage.

— Jeff Berke, CEO of Audience Acuity

Identity data has long been trapped behind complex query languages, forcing marketers to rely on technical teams to extract insights. Audience Acuity's new capability allows users to simply ask questions like "Which customer segments had the highest response rates to our last campaign?" or "What's the best channel mix to reach our high-value customers?" and receive instant, actionable answers with visualizations and strategic recommendations.

Before Conversational Identity Intelligence, getting answers from our identity data required submitting tickets to our analytics team with a 2-week turnaround. Now my team can explore identity insights in real-time during our strategy sessions, test hypotheses on the spot, and move directly from insights to activation. We've reduced our campaign planning cycle from weeks to days.

— Jane Smith, VP of Customer Acquisition at a Fortune 100 retailer

This isn't just a chat interface slapped onto an identity graph. We've built a true reasoning layer that understands both identity resolution concepts and strategic marketing objectives, creating a bridge between technical data and business outcomes.

— Jeff Sopko, Chief Growth Officer at Audience Acuity

Customer FAQs

Unlike general natural language query tools, Conversational Identity Intelligence is specifically designed for identity data. It understands complex identity concepts like match rates, identity resolution, cross-channel recognition, and audience activation. It's not just translating language to SQL—it's providing a reasoning layer that bridges identity data with marketing strategy.

No. Like all Audience Acuity services, Conversational Identity Intelligence operates entirely within your Snowflake environment. No data leaves your secure environment, maintaining complete data governance and privacy compliance.

Conversational Identity Intelligence is a new capability within our Super Identity Graph (SIG) that allows non-technical users to interact with identity data using natural language. Instead of needing to know SQL or rely on data analysts, marketers and strategists can simply ask questions and receive immediate answers, visualizations, and strategic recommendations.

You can ask discovery questions ("What segments have the highest match rates?"), comparative questions ("How do our high-value customers differ from occasional buyers?"), predictive questions ("Which channels would be most effective for reaching lapsed customers?"), and strategic questions ("How should I allocate my media budget across channels based on identity insights?").

No. While Conversational Identity Intelligence dramatically expands access to identity insights, data analysts remain essential for complex custom analyses, advanced modeling, and data governance. This tool empowers analysts to focus on higher-value work while democratizing access to everyday insights.

The system provides confidence scoring for every response and transparently explains its reasoning. For high-stakes decisions, the platform can generate the underlying SQL for analyst verification. Our early beta customers report accuracy comparable to analyst-generated queries for standard identity analytics tasks.

Conversational Identity Intelligence includes direct connectors to major DSPs, social platforms, email systems, and customer data platforms. You can seamlessly create and activate audiences based on your conversational analysis without switching contexts.

Since this capability is built natively into our Snowflake application, implementation is minimal. Most customers can be fully operational within days, including custom configuration for your specific identity data and business context.

Internal FAQs

This capability establishes a significant competitive moat by transforming our identity graph from a technical resource into a strategic business tool. No competitor currently offers natural language interaction with identity data, giving us a first-mover competitive advantage in the enterprise market.

This capability would be developed through a combination of internal resources and specialized Data Cloud with AI expertise. The total development time would be approximately 2 quarters from concept to GA, with 3 months of intensive beta testing with strategic customers.

Conversational Identity Intelligence expands our buyer persona beyond data teams to include marketing strategists, media planners, and business analysts. Sales teams should lead with business outcomes and strategic use cases rather than technical capabilities when presenting to these audiences.

Conversational Identity Intelligence is priced as a premium tier on top of our core SIG offering. We anticipate an increase in ARPU for customers who adopt this capability. The pricing could be structured as a monthly subscription based on user seats.

Support requirements are expected to be minimal after initial onboarding, as the system continuously improves through usage. Primary support needs will focus on advanced use case enablement rather than technical troubleshooting.

Initially, this capability is only available for Snowflake customers. Based on market reception, we'll evaluate expanding to other data cloud platforms in future releases. This Snowflake-first approach strengthens our strategic partnership and co-selling opportunities.

Key success metrics include: adoption rate within customer organizations, query volume by user role, time-to-insight compared to traditional methods, activation rate (percentage of queries leading to audience creation), and impact on renewal rates and expansion revenue.

Conversational Identity Intelligence represents a strategic expansion of the SIG platform from pure identity resolution to intelligence and activation. Future roadmap items could focus on expanding strategic capabilities, including user-driven and autonomous query and optimization, vertical specialization.
