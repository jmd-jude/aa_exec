Partnership Frameworks

Evaluating potential engagement models for our strategic collaboration.

Consulting

Project-based work with defined deliverables

Considerations:

  • Simple to implement
  • Limited long-term alignment
Joint Venture

Co-owned entity focused on specific innovations

Considerations:

  • Shared upside potential
  • IP ownership structure
Strategic Role Integration

Fractional or FT roles with base + growth incentives

Considerations:

  • Operational clarity
  • Balance of predictability and upside
